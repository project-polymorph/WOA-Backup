TRANS YOUTH

随着国门开放，去泰国手术比前两年方便了很多。

With the opening of the our country, it's much easier to go to Thailand for GAS (Gender-affirmation Surgery) than it was two years ago.

 

但是在泰国手术，就必须提供心理证明，这里我就来为大家介绍在拉玛9医院（Praram 9 Hospital）开具性别肯定手术心理证明的全攻略。

However, for GAS in Thailand, you must provide Medical Certificate, and here is my full guide to getting a Medical Certificate for GAS at Praram 9 Hospital.

  
   位置📍

Location📍

首先，拉玛9医院的地理位置就在：

First of all, Praram 9 Hospital is located:

เลขที่ 99 แขวงบางกะปิ เขตห้วยขวาง กรุงเทพฯ 10310

99 Rama IX Rd, Khwaeng Bang Kapi, Khet Huai Khwang, Krung Thep Maha Nakhon 10310

开心理证明的地方在B栋！

The place where the you can get the Medical Cerificate is in Building B (อาคาร B).


打车🚕

Ride-hailing🚕

你可以直接用inDrive、Bolt、Grab、Maxim等打车软件（按推荐优先级排序），输入目的地，一键前往。这些app都可以直接在国内的应用商店就能够下载到。Bolt可能需要进行护照认证。支付方式统统都可以付现金，也不建议绑卡（我19年Grab绑卡然后就被盗刷了）。准备一些100元面值的泰铢就可以了，司机基本上都能够找开。ATM机取到的都是1000元面值的，可以在711买东西找零。

You can directly use Ride-hailing apps like inDrive, Bolt, Grab, Maxim, etc. (listed in the recommended order of preference), input the destination, and go with one click. These apps can be downloaded directly from the app store in China. Some App may require passport verification. For payment, you can pay with cash in all of them, and it is not recommended to link a card(I linked a card with Grab in 2019 and I have identity theft on my card). Prepare some 100 Thai Baht notes, and drivers can basically make change. ATMs dispense 1,000 Baht notes, which can be changed at 711 stores by making purchases.

Grab价格最贵，比其他软件高出2倍。inDrive界面只能设置为英文，但是是我在曼谷几天体验下来打车最清爽的App了，选择From出发地和To目的地之后，发起订单，是一个非常便宜的价格，然后这个价格可能不太容易找到司机（Bolt就是这样，几乎找不到车），但是这个app的好处是，司机可以向你发送一个价格，如果你点击接受，那么就可以立刻找到车了。而且这个价格也是比较低的。Maxim的话，地址输入比较麻烦，需要在地图上选点，而不是输入地址，最不推荐。

The price for Grab is the most expensive, about twice as high as other apps. The inDrive interface can only be set in English, but it is the best app I've experienced in a few days in Bangkok. After selecting the From departure point and To destination, starting an order, it is at a very cheap price. Then, it might not be easy to find a driver at this price (Bolt is like this, it's hard to find a car), but the good thing about this app is, the driver can send you a price. If you click accept, you can find a car immediately. And this price is also fairly low. As for Maxim, inputting an address is cumbersome, it requires selecting a point on the map rather than entering an address, I would not recommend it.


轨道交通🚇

BTS SKY TRAIN🚇

曼谷的轨道交通相对打车来说真的是极致的省钱和快速。在曼谷有一次去王权免税店在路上堵了3个小时。但是如果坐轨道交通就完全不会堵车。但是相对来说，轨道交通也不是没有缺点，比如附近没有站点啦，购票麻烦等。

The BTS system in Bangkok is ultimately a real money and time saver compared to taking taxis. Once, I got stuck in traffic for 3 hours en route to the King Power Duty Free Store in Bangkok. However, if you take the BTS, you will definitely not experience traffic. Nevertheless, there are also some downsides to the BTS, such as the lack of nearby stations, and the inconvenience of purchasing tickets.

 

有一说一，曼谷的轨道交通购票是真的麻烦，在自动售票机上买票，不管你是投币还是纸钞，疯狂不吞钞（包括自助洗衣店的投币机也是这样），让人抓狂。还是直接去人工窗口排队，找客服说站点名和告诉她几个人就行。当然如果注册了Line，并且有在Line Pay中绑定了Visa/万事达的卡片，那么自动售票机绝对是最方便的。

Honestly, purchasing tickets for Bangkok's BTS is really a hassle. Whether you're inserting coins or banknotes into the ticket vending machines, it frustratingly does not accept them (the same issue occurs at coin laundries). It's better to queue at the ticket counter, tell the customer service officer your destination and the number of passengers. Of course, if you have registered for Line and bound your Visa/MasterCard in Line Pay, then the ticket vending machine becomes the most convenient option.

 

离拉玛9医院的站点是Bule Line（蓝线）的Phra Ram 9（帕蓝9）和Phetchaburi（碧差汶），都是1.4公里。

The station nearest to Praram 9 Hospital is either Phra Ram 9 or Phetchaburi Station on the Blue Line, each about 1.4 kilometers away.

医院🏥

流程🧾

Process🧾

到达医院B栋（Building B），首先需要先在1楼大厅进行信息登记，并告知你进行性别肯定手术（Gender-affirmation surgery）需要两份心理证明（Medical Certificate），如果听不懂也没关系，可以直接跟前台说“Need Chinese Translator”【空耳：尼德 拆尼子 穿丝累特】。然后用跟翻译说你做性别肯定手术需要心理证明（医院的翻译水平参差不齐，可能不能理解“性别肯定手术”可以说“变性手术”）。

Upon arriving at Building B of the hospital, you need to first register your information on the 1st floor lobby, and inform them that you need two medical certificates for gender affirmation surgery. If you don't understand, it's okay, you can directly say to the receptionist "Need Chinese Translator"【/niːd/ /ˌtʃaɪˈniːz/ /trænsˈleɪtər/】.

然后会让你登记信息，需要用到护照，填写你的姓名、护照号码、电话、邮箱、在泰国的地址等等信息。

Then tell the translator that you need a psychological certificate for the gender affirmation surgery. Next, they will have you register your information, which involves providing your passport, filling out your name, passport number, phone, email, address in Thailand, etc. 

登记完了会让你上9楼，坐电梯上到9楼之后，在右手边找到Mind Center，然后继续跟前台说出你的需求。

Once registration is complete, they will have you go to the 9th floor. After taking the elevator to the 9th floor, find the Mind Center on the right hand side, and then continue to state your needs to the front desk.


然后前台的护士小姐姐or翻译首先会带你进行体检，测量身高、体重和血压，然后帮你让你坐下来等待心理医生。等到护士叫你的时候，跟着翻译一起进去诊室，然后和医生沟通，医生会问一些问题来了解你的情况。翻译会帮助你翻译医生的每个问题，也会把你的回答翻译成泰语告诉给医生。翻译的中文水平可能不是非常好，例如在我就诊时，她就将“手术后不能后悔，不能detransition”翻译为“还回去”，让我困惑了好久。

Then, the front desk nurse or translator will first take you for a physical examination, measuring your height, weight and blood pressure, and then ask you to sit down and wait for the psychologist. When the nurse calls you, follow the translator into the examination room, and then communicate with the doctor. The doctor will ask some questions to understand your situation. The translator will help translate each of the doctor's questions and convey your answers to the doctor in Thai.

 

与国内程序相比，在拉玛九医院获取心理证明更为简便。无需准备繁杂的文档，无需提供推荐信或介绍信，不需要父母的同意信和公证信函，不需要做一大堆的量表，也无需进行染色体检测，更无需经历长期的评估或进行多次复诊。

Compared to the procedures in China, obtaining a Medical Certificate at Praram 9 Hospital is more straightforward. There’s no need to prepare complex documentation, provide recommendation letters or introductory letters, parental consent letters, notarization letters, complete numerous scales, nor undergo chromosome tests, or go through lengthy assessments or multiple follow-ups.

 

医生的职责也并非确定您是否为跨性别者，或是否必须接受手术。医生的角色是评估您是否有能力做出知情同意的决定，以及能否使用相关的性别认证医疗信息来指导您的医疗决策。医生还将评估任何可能影响性别肯定医疗干预结果的身心健康条件，并与您一起讨论手术的风险。

The doctor's role is not to determine whether you are transgender or whether you must undergo surgery. Their role is to assess whether you have the ability to make an informed consent decision, and whether you can use related gender certification medical information to guide your medical decisions. The doctor will also evaluate any mental and physical health conditions that may affect the outcome of gender affirmation medical interventions, and discuss risks of the surgery with you.

 

等到看完了两个医生之后，会给你一张单子，让你去10楼的Cashier缴费（和国内医院一样的，药房Pharmacy旁边就是缴费窗口），微信和支付宝都可以用，像是国内医院一样。

After seeing two doctors, you will receive a form which directs you to the 10th floor Cashier to pay (similar to hospitals in China, the payment window is located next to the Pharmacy). Both WeChat and Alipay are accepted, just like in Chinese hospitals. 


总的花费是，两位医生一共3600泰铢，再加上一个Other Medical Service（应该是翻译或者是体检的费用250泰铢）总共是3850泰铢。按照今天的汇率大概是800块人民币。

The total cost is 3600 Thai Baht for two doctors, plus an Other Medical Service (probably translation or physical examination fee of 250 Thai Baht), totaling 3850 Thai Baht. According to today's exchange rate, it is about 800 CNY.

 

缴完费之后就会得到一张跟上图一样的盖有PAID的缴费单据，拿到单据之后回到9楼，将单据交给前台护士就可以领到2封心理证明啦（每封心理证明都有2份，手术会收走一份原件）。

After payment, you will receive a receipt like the one shown above, marked as PAID. Once you have the receipt, return to the 9th floor and give it to the receptionist to receive two psychological certificates (each certificate has two copies, one original will be taken away for surgery).

有了这个证明就可以在泰国找医生&医院进行性别肯定手术啦。

之后我们也会根据本文的反馈出一些各个医院的流程和篇章【不含对医生技术主观评价】

With this certificate, you can seek gender-affirmation surgery in Thailand. Later, we will also release some processes and articles about various hospitals according to the feedback of this article (excluding subjective evaluations of doctor's skills).


一些需要注意的问题🛑

1.一定需要带上护照，如果未满20周岁（泰国成年年龄）则可能需要父母一方到场签字确认。

2.医院提供翻译的时间段为7:00 - 23:00（更多语种的翻译请咨询医院，联系方式在最后）

3.最好16:00之前到达医院，这样可以在一天之内看完两名医生。

Issues to be mindful of 🛑:

1. It is absolutely necessary to bring your passport. If you are under 20 years old (Thai adult age), one parent may need to show up to sign a confirmation.

2. The hospital provides translation services from 7:00 - 23:00 (for translations in more languages, please consult the hospital, contact details are at the end)

3. It is better to arrive at the hospital before 16:00 so you can see two doctors within one day.

（完）
